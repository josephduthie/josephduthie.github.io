# Bio
I am a second year mathematics DPhil (PhD) student at the University of Oxford, working under the supervision of Professor Jason Lotay.

My research is in the areas of differential geometry and geometric analysis. In particular, I am interested in geometric flows and manifolds with exceptional holonomy - you can find out more about it [here](/publications/).
